    Quarter vs semester credits (QCH ↔ SCH)

    QCH
    Quarter credit hours
    Maestro College's unit. The AAS runs on quarter terms — every course on the left is shown in QCH.
    SCH
    Semester credit hours
    The University's unit. The A.S./B.S. and every course on the right is in SCH — the A.S. is 60 SCH.
    Conversion
    1 SCH ≈ 1.5 QCH
    6 QCH ≈ 4 SCH · 4 QCH ≈ 2.67 SCH.

    Transfer is decided course-to-course, not by credit total: one College course clears at most one University course, whatever each is worth. That's why a 6-QCH College course yields only the University course's 3 SCH — the leftover (≈1.5 QCH) shows up as conversion shrinkage in “wasted credit.” A College course with no University match is lost outright; one that matches a course above the A.S. counts ↗ toward a B.S.
